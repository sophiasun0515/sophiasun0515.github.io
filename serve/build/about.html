About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student in computer engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology

I am passionate about work that pushes the boundaries of human sensory experiences and bridges the gap between the physical and digital world - making interactions with the cyber world more intuitive and a natural extension of the physical world. I work with different technologies to engineer such experiences with a strong focus on human-centered design, making these applications contextually relevant.

Currently, I am exploring IoT and signal processing applications. I am also looking forward to experimenting with machine learning, computer vision and bioengineering in the near future. I love bringing together diverse fields and believe that it is this intersection that creates magic.
